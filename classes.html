Extend

Extend is a seated exercise class for those who prefer lighter exercise. It is aimed at the over 60s and those who struggle with mobility. The class is friendly and helpful, and they meet for coffee and biscuits after each session.

Please ring 07837 971147 or pop into the cafe to book your place.

Coffee that Cares

Are you a carer? Loving and caring for a person with special needs can be an isolating experience. You are not alone! This group provides a safe space for you to relax, find friendship and share advice with other people who share some of the everyday trials and triumphs you face.

Please ring 07837 971147 or pop into the cafe to book your place.

Foodbank

Over 2 million people in teh UK need a little help feeding their families. If this is you, then we are here to help! Click on the button below to sign up for free foodbank vouchers, then bring your vouchers along on Thursdays and help yourself to a wide variety of yummy food!

Community Pantry

Are you hungry? You do not need to register or qualify to eat food from the Community Pantry. This food is not guaranteed in any way and may be past its sell by date. This is food given with love by people who care that you get enough to eat. Take what you need.

Are you a food business owner? Please don't throw out the food you can't sell tomorrow. Here at the Community Pantry, we will make sure it gets to someone who needs it. Please ring 07837 971147 or pop into the cafe to let us know if we can help you donate in any way.
