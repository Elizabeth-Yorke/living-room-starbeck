Our Ethos

Here at Living Room Starbeck we firmly believe that:

You can't help everyone, but everyone can help someone.

We do our best every day to help as many people as possible in as many ways as possible.

If you have an idea of something you can do to help someone, and you think that our cafe could help in any way, please come and speak to one of our staff members. We will do everything we can to help you!

History

Living Room Starbeck opened in 2005. We are a community cafe run on volunteer power. We are supported by the Life Destiny Church, Foodbank, and a number of other great organisations who want to help our community thrive.
