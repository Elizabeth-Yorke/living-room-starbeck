By community, for community, in community!

Our Ethos

Here at Living Room Starbeck we firmly believe that:

You can't help everyone, but everyone can help someone.

We do our best every day to help as many people as possible in as many ways as possible.

If you have an idea of something you can do to help someone, and you think that our cafe could help in any way, please come and speak to one of our staff members. We will do everything we can to help you!

Awards

Since Living Room Starbeck opened, we have always been a community cafe run on volunteer power. We are supported by the Life Destiny Church, Foodbank, and a number of other great organisations and people who want to help our community thrive. Because of the fantastic support we recieve from our community, we have grown from strength to strength, and we hope to continue doing so. In recognisition of the hard work put in by all the members of our community, we have recieved a number of awards. We would like to take this opportunity to thank everyone for the hard work they put in every day.

Charity Collections

At Living Room Starbeck we support a wide variety of charities. If you have anything you think you would like to donate to someone who needs it (particularly clothes, food, toys, etc), please let us know. We will do our best to see that it reaches someone who really needs it.

Volunteering

Do you have free time or skills that you would to use to help somebody else? Please come into the cafe and speak to one of us! We can't wait to hear from you!
